ABOUT ME

I then moved to Andover, where I reside today. For most of my life, I wasn't into coding, but I've always had an interest in technology and how things work. But during sophomore year of high school, I took a Minecraft programming class and was instantly hooked. Since then, I’ve taken numerous CS courses, including AP Computer Science Java and AP Computer Science Principles. I will major in Computer Science in college. My other interests include track and field and cross country. I’ve competed in varsity track for three years, and varsity cross country for two, and I am captain of both teams.

I have also worked at Brooks School Summer Programs for two years as a counselor.
